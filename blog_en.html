Our Story

Once upon a time, two BTG students who were in the 6th semester class at QB1 did not speak much to each other, but they wanted to get together to be able to carry out a micro-enterprise in which they did not know what they were getting into. Watching some videos on TikTok, they came up with the great idea of ​​making and selling marquesitas and frappuccinos. This was something new and exciting for both of them, but they wanted to take the risk.

They began to organize and plan how they wanted the project to be successful. In the first sale they were too nervous about not knowing how it would work and how many customers they could have, but everything was chaos since they never thought that several people would want to buy and try the product; They began to prepare various orders that they were making, but the machine did not help them since it was very slow to make the marquesita and apart from that it burned and stuck and they did not manage to get the marquesitas to turn out well, so they could only sell the frappuccinos.

While they were selling they felt stressed, angry, sad, angry that things were not going as planned. They continue to feel disappointed by the results obtained, but in spite of that they keep going and trying to improve more and more.
