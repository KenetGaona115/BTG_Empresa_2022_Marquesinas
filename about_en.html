About our enterprise

Mission

We are a micro-enterprise that sells marquesitas and oreo and mocha frappuccinos; with good prices, good production and good cleaning.

Vision

To be (by May/2022) in the sale of marquesitas and frappuccinos the only micro-company that sells good marquesitas and the best frappuccinos, also having a good image of the business, good capital and also a good recommendation by all our clients.

Objectives

1. As a first objective we want to take out everything that was given as capital.
2. Have good sales.
3. That it be a micro-enterprise with good profits to satisfy our personal expenses.
4. Approve all the points of the project in order to get a good grade.
